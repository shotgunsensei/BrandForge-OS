You are continuing development of an existing SaaS platform called BrandForge OS.

BrandForge OS is a multi-tenant AI-powered marketing operating system for businesses, founders, agencies, and multi-brand teams. The app already has:
- a strong MVP foundation
- onboarding
- brand management
- AI workflows
- copy generation
- creative studio
- campaign builder
- landing page builder
- content calendar
- analytics dashboards
- billing foundations
- multi-tenant architecture

Your job now is to execute Phase 3: monetization, integrations, reporting, publishing, marketplace foundations, and revenue-driving product improvements that make BrandForge OS feel commercially viable and genuinely launchable.

IMPORTANT:
- Do not rebuild the product from scratch
- Preserve the best existing architecture and improve it
- Refactor only where needed for clarity, monetization, scalability, or cleaner integrations
- Prioritize real business value, real upgrade triggers, and real operational usefulness
- Make the platform feel like it can actually be sold to customers
