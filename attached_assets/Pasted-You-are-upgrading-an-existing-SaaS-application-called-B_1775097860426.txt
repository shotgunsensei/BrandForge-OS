Your job now is to execute Phase 2 and final MVP polish so the product feels premium, coherent, monetizable, and much closer to launch readiness.

IMPORTANT:
Do not rebuild this from scratch unless necessary.

Refactor weak areas only when required for stability, clarity, scalability, or UX quality.
